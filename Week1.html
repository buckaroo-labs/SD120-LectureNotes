Slide 1-01

Why?

Yes, this is a Software Development course (and it used to be a Computer Science course), but it is also a course about a foundation of communication in the modern world.

We'll be writing a lot of code, but we will always be doing so with the goal of communicating something.

Web design and web programming have become relevant to countless aspects of modern life.

As you might have noticed, this presentation is not a PowerPoint slide deck; it's running in a browser. Everything on the screen is written in HTML, styled with CSS and managed by JavaScript.

Your ability to participate and communicate in the modern world can be enhanced by your ability to create web content, and your ability to analyze and transform content created by others can give you a greater degree of control in your experience of an increasingly-online world.
